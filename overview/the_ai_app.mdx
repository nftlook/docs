**3look connects brands and creators through campaigns that reward original content.**

Brands launch campaigns with defined rules and budgets.\
Creators join, create content, and publish from their own social accounts.\
Posts that meet campaign requirements and generate qualified engagement earn rewards.

Activity is tracked automatically.\
Rewards accumulate and are claimable every 24 hours.

Brands allocate budget for attention.\
Creators earn by driving it.

**Join a campaign → Post → Claim rewards**

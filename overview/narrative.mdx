## What is Narrative Relevance?

Narrative relevance measures how well your content matches the** Key Narratives** of a campaign.

Each campaign defines a set of ideas, themes, and messages that your content is expected to reflect.

The closer your post aligns with these narratives, the higher its relevance, and the higher your reward.

---

### How It Works

When you publish a campaign post the system looks for:

- Meaningful use of campaign-related concepts
- Clear alignment with the campaign message
- Contextually relevant wording (not just keywords)

Using isolated words or mentions is not enough.\
Your content needs to communicate the narrative.

---

### What High Narrative Relevance Looks Like 

### (Do this)

High-performing posts:

- Clearly reflect the** Key Narratives** of the campaign
- Communicate the main idea or message behind the campaign
- Combine multiple relevant points into a coherent message
- Feel natural and contextually accurate

Strong posts don’t just mention the brand, they explain or reinforce what the campaign is about.

---

### What Low Narrative Relevance Looks Like 

### (Don’t do this)

Posts with low relevance typically:

- Only mention the brand without context
- Use keywords without meaningful explanation
- Miss the core idea of the campaign
- Feel generic or disconnected

These posts may still be tracked, but they receive significantly lower rewards.

---

### **Best Practices**

To maximize your narrative relevance:

- Focus on the campaign’s **Key Narratives**
- Explain, don’t just mention
- Combine multiple ideas into a single post
- Keep your content clear and intentional

Quality matters more than volume.

---

### **Posting Frequency**

Posting more ≠ more rewards.

Each campaign may have its own daily posting limits, but in general:

- Focus on up to 2 high-quality posts per day
- Additional posts may have reduced impact or may not be counted
- Excessive posting can be flagged as spam or low-effort (including AI-generated slop) and may lead to disqualification from the campaign

Quality and relevance matter more than volume.

Focus on fewer, stronger posts that clearly match the campaign narrative.

---

### **Key Takeaway**

Narrative relevance is about alignment, not keywords.

The better your content reflects the campaign’s core messages,\
the higher its impact, and the more you earn.

## What are Creator Tiers?

**Creator Tiers** represent your status on 3look, based on your performance as a creator.

They reflect how well your content performs, how relevant your audience is, and how you compare to other creators on the platform.

**Your tier determines what campaigns and opportunities you can access.**

Each creator is assigned a **tier and a score**, which update over time based on performance.

---

### Tier Breakdown

**Under Review**\
Your account is being evaluated.\
You're not yet eligible to participate in campaigns.\
Accounts are automatically reviewed every two weeks for potential access.

**Iron**\
Emerging creators starting to build relevant activity and engagement.

**Bronze**\
Notable creators with consistent participation and early traction.

**Silver**\
Established creators with proven performance and audience relevance.

**Gold**\
Strong creators with consistent results across campaigns.

**Platinum**\
Top-performing creators with high-quality content and reliable impact.

**Diamond**\
Exceptional creators with outstanding performance and influence.

---

### How Tier Progression Works

You grow your tier through a combination of account quality and campaign performance.

**Performance is evaluated based on:**

- Content quality
- Qualified engagement from relevant audiences
- Overall account strength and audience relevance

Participating in campaigns helps establish your performance, but tier progression depends on how your content performs and who engages with it.

Stronger accounts with relevant audiences and consistent high-quality engagement move up faster, unlocking access to more campaigns and opportunities.

---

### Creator Score

Creator Score is 3look’s native rating that reflects your performance as a creator.

It evaluates the quality of your content, the relevance of your audience, and how your posts perform across campaigns.

This score updates over time and determines your **Creator Tier.**

---

### Where to Check Your Tier

Your Creator Tier and level are assigned when you join 3look.

You can track your current status and progress in your profile section.

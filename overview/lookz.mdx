## What are Løøkz?

Løøkz are 3look-native reward points.

They are distributed in 3look-run campaigns and reflect your performance within those campaigns.

Not all campaigns reward Løøkz.\
Brand campaigns may distribute their own points or crypto rewards instead.

Løøkz balances update every 24 hours when rewards are distributed.

### What are Løøkz used for?

Løøkz represent your contribution and performance in 3look-native campaigns.

They can:

- Increase your eligibility for 3look-native on-chain rewards
- Strengthen your position in future ecosystem distributions
- Signal early participation and sustained impact
- Improve visibility for campaign opportunities that prioritize proven creators

Løøkz are earned based on the performance of your eligible campaign posts and qualified engagement.

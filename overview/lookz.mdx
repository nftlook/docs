Løøkz are 3look-native reward points that reflect your social content’s impact.

You earn them based on engagement of your posts and replies sent through 3look

Your  Løøkz balance helps you unlock key benefits:

- On-chain reward payouts
- Leaderboard rankings
- Access to exclusive campaigns, content & extra features

The more relevant and engaging your content, the more Løøkz you’ll earn.
